Hip Hop Dance


Hip hop dance is a high-energy, urban dance style that incorporates a variety of styles including breaking, popping, locking, and more. In this class, you'll learn the fundamental techniques of hip hop dance while also exploring your own unique style. Our experienced instructors will guide you through a series of fun and challenging routines that will improve your coordination, strength, and flexibility.
